Twik: Onboarding

Set up Twik on Your Landing Page.

Optimizing your website does not have to be a big deal. Twik offers a simple set up and an elaborate analytics and BI dashboard.  To begin optimizing your site, you can start using Twik in just a few simple steps:

  • Sign in through Google or Facebook to create an account
  • Insert your website URL
  • Save the changes
  • Wild Card – if you want Twik to be active on a level above the root folder, or all levels above,  click on “properties”, choose the wild card tab, and add asterisks accordingly.
  • Copy the snippet and insert to the head tag of your website’s code.

Now it’s experimenting time. Read our article “Creating Experiments” to learn more about experiments.
