How can I upgrade?

You can upgrade your Twik account at any point. Simply select the plan you wish to switch to and confirm your payment details. Your account renewal date will update accordingly.

Absolutely not. Twik is a monthly subscription service. You are obligated to comply with our terms of service, but will never be asked to sign a contract or be charged any hidden fees. Use Twik as much as you want and stop at any time.
