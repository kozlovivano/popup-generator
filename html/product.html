Audiences

Tap into your audience

Variants

Tweak every element

Variants are pieces of content, such as text, image, destination URL, and color, that can be deployed as experiments onto different selectors. There are 9 types of variants that can be used across tens of selectors. For example, a ‘color’ variant can be used to change text selectors and background selector colors.

Text

Chose specific text to reflect site-wide in headlines, paragraphs, anchor texts, titles, placeholders and button URLs.

Text format

Change the styling of the text throughout the website, including font family, weight, size, alignment, and more.

Image

Upload images, including background images, and have them displayed throughout your website.

Color

Assign color values to any kind of text, and change the background color of any section on your website.

Dynamic variant

Display dynamic content by selecting a pre-defined selector as a variant.

Custom HTML

Replace the markup on your website with custom HTML.

Custom JavaScript

Run custom scripts to change the behavior of different elements across your website.

Custom CSS

Use custom styling to easily change the style of elements across your website.

Visual editor

Edit & preview experiments

Our visual editor is unlike any other. Without setup requirements or preliminary implementations, all you have to do is enter a URL and a replica of the page will be displayed. This is where the fun begins and you can start experimenting with images, texts, colors and more. Select the element you want personalize, add the variants, choose your audience, preview the new page, and save to deploy the experiments.

Selectors

When clicking on elements within our visual editor, you’ll be shown a drop-down menu with attributes that can be edited for that element (for example, when clicking on a link, you’ll be shown a menu enabling you to change its caption text, font style and destination URL); The combination of an element (ie. Link) and the selected attribute (ie. Destination URL, Font style or Caption text) make a ‘Selector’.

Dashboard & BI

Unlock new insights

Personalize and analyze with a single-page application. Twik’s dashboard includes side panel navigation where you can easily shift from one platform view to another. With built in analytics, you can segment data, compare performance results, and review BI. Visualize the effects of the experiments by retrieving graphs and table data, comparing the original site to the personalized site.

Pages

Pages represent the different URLs within a specific project. Pages are being indexed using our JS snippet and will automatically appear on your dashboard with their correlating statistics. If you wish to personalize a page were traffic hasn’t been recorded yet, simply press the green plus button under the ‘Pages’ view, and enter the page URL you wish to personalize.

Add-ons & integrations

We created several add-ons to enable you with simple integrations of Twik to common platforms.

Wordpress plugin

A simple integration of Twik into WordPress websites gives you the ability to automatically send conversions data to CRMs and select which pages you wish to run Twik on. The plugin eliminates the need for a markup snippet with a simple 'snippet-id to input' setup.

Google Ads extension

Twik selectors act as live URL parameters. Our Chrome extension enhances Google Ads with additional capabilities, enabling you to inject variants to specfic Twik selectors via the ad tracking-template, without the need to create new ads or affect the ad history. Improve quality scores and lower campaign CPCs by increasing ads to landing page relevancy at a click of a button.

Digital Signage Addon

In an effort to bring our personalization capabilities to the real world, we have partnered with NoviSign, a leading digital signage SaaS provider to create a digital signage personalization solution. NoviSign's signage solution has a built-in face recognition ability that can operate with a simple webcam. Combined with Twik, this allows you to personalize your screen messages according to the age & gender of the person who looks at your monitor.

API

Twik runs a JSON file at the background of each user session, enabling you to easily tap into it, extract data and enhance your own DB with its content. The information on file contains user behavioural & demographical data along with our unique fingerprint ID, so essentially, you could use it to stop using cookies of your own.
